# {{ title }} 

<img src="/content-media/images/sain-logo.webp" alt="SAIN" class="article-image-header" style="width: 50%; margin-bottom: 1em;" />

The [Strategic AI Network (SAIN)](https://ics.ie/sain/) is a special interest group within the [Irish Computer Society (ICS)](https://ics.ie/). It has been setup as a collaborative professional community focused on the responsible, ethical and effective use of artificial intelligence.

As of March 2026, I am delighted to join the SAIN executive committee. I'm looking forward to contributing to the work of the group, and collaborating with members of the Irish Computer Society on AI-related initiatives.
